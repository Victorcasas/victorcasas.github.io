CouchDB and Vagrant

Jul 25 2014 development

The default for CouchDB is listening on address 127.0.0.1 of the virtual machine, not the physical machine.

Modify your /etc/couchdb/local.ini like this:

[httpd] bind_address = 0.0.0.0

Restart CouchDB and check in you browser:

http://127.0.0.1:5984/_utils/index.html
