Designing a RubyMotion iOS App

Nov 29 2014 ios development

As I said in my previous post, My first RubyMotion app for iOS, I decided to do a simple application to know how much I everyday spend in coffee, buying food, dinning out, taxi, etc.

It's my first RubyMotion application so I plan to code only essential features to write not many code. I want to mantain updated the app and I want to refactor without many efforts to improve the code with new stuff I learn.

Besides, if I want to use the application everyday it has to be useful and easy to use. The idea is simple: I know how much I pay every month in rent or energy but I don't know how many times I lunch out of the office and how much I spend.

Definition of Home Screen

This is the screen that I'll watch everytime I open the app so it must contains useful information and buttons to do most used actions.

Navigation Bar

  • Left, typical icon to slide a menu with more options
  • Right, plus icon to create a new spend

Body

  • Big total day
  • Total week
  • Total month
  • Customizable value
  • List of last spends

Prototyping in paper

Before get the pencil I did a lot of research in App Store and Pttrns website to look for inspiration, colors, ui, ux and simply and clever apps.

In previous sketchs I made I put my mobile up papel and round it with a pencil but this time I've used a cool tool, courtesy of UI Stencils. It helps me to draw faster and better my sketchs. It's really useful!

Prototyping in Sketch 3

Finished prototype in paper next step it's to design it in Sketch 3 an application that surprised me in a good way. It's that I need to prototype and design iOS apps, websites, etc. It has cool features like @2x export option.

It includes templates to do iOS App icons or iOS UI Designs. Also, there's a lot of resources and plugins at Sketch App Sources.

Morever, there is a iOS app called Sketch Mirror that allows you to preview your design live in your device connecting it to your computer and clicking in Mirror button in Sketch 3.

Finally and after work for a while this is like Home Screen looks. You can download the design from Home Screen Sketch file. Also, you can download the iPhone design from iPhone 6 Sketch file.

Next step is code it!
